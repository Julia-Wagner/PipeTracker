Introduction for new users

PipeTracker is a powerful inventory management application designed specifically for plumbers. Use it to improve your workflow and make tracking your inventory easier. You can structure your stock items in categories and manage delivery notes to simplify your invoicing process. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Explore the Dashboard
    • Take a moment to explore the dashboard, above the introduction you´ll find your real-time statistics.
  2. Stock Management
    • Open Stock Items in the menu to efficiently manage your inventory.
    • Organize items into categories and subcategories for easy accessibility.
    • When you open a category with stock items, you can order the table based on your preferred criteria.
    • Search for the desired stock item by name, size or matchcode.
    • Use the QR code in the item detail page to print out for your physical stock to quickly open the desired item.
    • Easily update item quantities with a simple click of the - or + buttons.
    • Automatically create stock items by uploading a CSV file following the structure of the example file.
  3. Basket
    • Use the basket to collect stock items you want to add to a delivery note.
    • No need to assign a delivery note for each item – simply gather them in the basket.
    • Add items to the basket directly from the stock items table by specifying the quantity and clicking Add.
    • You can also update the quantity in the basket, the stock item gets updated automatically.
  4. Delivery Notes
    • Create and manage multiple delivery notes as needed.
    • Choose an existing customer or create a new one when adding a delivery note.
    • When you are done with a delivery note, you can mark it as closed.
    • Add items to your delivery note directly from your basket by selecting the desired note.
    • Export your delivery note and save it to a PDF file.
